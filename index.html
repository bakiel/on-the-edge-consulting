Who We Are

Advisory built at the edge. Strategy rooted in purpose.

On The Edge Consulting is a boutique ESG and sustainability advisory firm based in Roodepoort, Gauteng. We guide businesses across Sub-Saharan Africa and beyond through the intersection of regulatory compliance, stakeholder expectation, and long-term value creation.

Our work is precise, practical, and deeply rooted in the belief that sustainable business is simply good business - and that the organisations who lead on ESG will define the next decade of global commerce.

What We Do

Four pillars.
One direction.

Focused advisory across the full ESG spectrum - from compliance architecture to cultural transformation.

01

ESG Strategy & Reporting

Integrated ESG frameworks aligned with GRI, SASB, and TCFD standards. From materiality assessments to sustainability reports that satisfy investors, funders, and regulators - built for the Sub-Saharan African and global context.

02

Regulatory Compliance

Navigating the evolving environmental and social legislative landscape - NEMA, B-BBEE, King IV, and regional equivalents. We translate compliance obligations into strategic advantage, not just administrative burden.

03

Stakeholder Engagement

Communities, investors, boards, and regulators each require a different conversation. We craft and facilitate stakeholder engagement strategies that build genuine trust and durable license to operate.

04

Sustainability Training

Equipping leadership and teams with the knowledge to embed sustainability thinking across the organisation. Practical, contextual, and built for the realities of the evolving global landscape.
